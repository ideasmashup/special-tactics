Objectives

The main goal of the project is to build a decent AI for a fast-paced strategic game like Starcraft. Our team primarily consists of RTS veterans who are familiar with the game mechanics and decision-making processes.

However, turning that instinctive human play into a computer program is no small task so we intend to improve the AI over time, little by little.

To evaluate and assess progress, we will register our bot to the SSCAI servers so that it can compete with other bots and climb up the rankings along with code improvements
